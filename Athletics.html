Athletics at Miami University

Miami University of Ohio athletes perform at a high level in multiple sports such as football, basketball, ice hockey, and track and field. With 50+ dedicated club teams, Miami Club Sports boasts one of the largest programs in the country. While some club sports have coaches, most are completely student-driven with advisement from coaches, advisers, and the Club Sports Office. Students support students, creating a supportive environment.

STUDENT ATHLETE LIFE

The athletic department has various sports counselors/advisors that stay up to date with the athletes well being and walk to academic achievements. There are multiple leadership positions that create a welcoming environment for athletes to get the help they need while playing their sport. Whether an athlete's long-term goal is to go pro or not, advisors ensure that there is a plan to succeed on their path to their degree. Because playing a college sport can be overwhelming, advisors work with students to create a personalized plan to balance life overall as an athlete; personal life, academics, etc. Miami University prioritized athletes' mental health and psychological state. This creates a headspace where a student can perform their best. Having the support system of an athletic department can be detrimental to an athlete's journey.
