About Us

Miami University is a public research university in Oxford, Ohio, United States. Founded in 1809, it is the second-oldest university in Ohio and the tenth-oldest public university in the United States.

  • infoWhat is campus life like?
    Whether you want to enhance your passions or explore new opportunities, our vibrant campus life offers you a wide variety of ways to get involved. With more than 50 student organizations and competitive collegiate athletics, there's bound to be something for everyone.
  • schoolWhere can I find out what courses are offered?
    The most up-to-date course information is online at www.mymiami.MiamiOH.edu. You can search for courses using parameters like campus, subject, course number, part of term, or instructor.
  • scheduleHow many hours should I take?
    To be considered a full-time student, register for at least 12 hours. Most students take between 14 and 18 hours. A maximum of 17 (or 18 if you're in engineering) is allowed initially.
